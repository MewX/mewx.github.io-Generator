　　Olaf’s Frozen Adventure is a 2017 American 3D computer-animated holiday-themed musical comedy short film directed by Kevin Deters and Stevie Wermers, released by Walt Disney Pictures. The film stars Josh Gad, Kristen Bell, Idina Menzel, Jonathan Groff.
