Demon's Souls is an IP developed by Sony & Fromsoftware, that brought back the medieval setting of the King's Field series.

Announced as a Playstation 3 exclusive at TGS 2008, the game bought Fromsoftware & Sony Japan studios . The game is an Action RPG with focus on strategic combat choices, offering melee and ranged mechanics as well as magic gameplay.

Bluepoint Games, the developers behind the PS4 remake of Shadow of the Colossus, has announced its new PS5 game, a remake of the beloved FromSoftware PS3 exclusive, Demon's Souls.
