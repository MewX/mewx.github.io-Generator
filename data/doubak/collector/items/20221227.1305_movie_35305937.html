(展开全部) 　　The 2021 Vienna Philharmonic New Year's Concert takes place on January 1, 2021, under the baton of Riccardo Muti in the Golden Hall of the Musikverein in Vienna. After 1993, 1997, 2000, 2004, and 2018, this year's concert will mark the 6th time that Riccardo Muti, whose close musical partnership with the Vienna Philharmonic has now extended nearly 50 years, conducts this prestigious concert event.
　　The 2021 New Year's Concert will be broadcast in over 90 countries and followed by millions of television viewers around the world. The 2021 New Year's Concert will take place without an audience.
