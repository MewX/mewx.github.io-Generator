简介  · · · · · ·

Tom Clancy's The Division is an upcoming massively multiplayer online third-person shooter video game developed by Ubisoft Massive, Ubisoft Reflections and Ubisoft Red Storm under the Tom Clancy brand for Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 4 and Xbox One. It was announced during Ubisoft's E3 2013 press conference, together with a seven minute gameplay demo. The game is set to release in 2015.
